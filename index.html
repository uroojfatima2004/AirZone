About AirZone

AirZone is a comprehensive air quality monitoring platform that provides real-time data and analytics to help you understand your environment better.

Our system collects data from multiple sensors and processes it to give you actionable insights about air quality, temperature, humidity, and various pollutants.

With AirZone, you can make informed decisions about outdoor activities, ventilation needs, and health precautions based on accurate, up-to-date information.
